Concept&Experience

Intended concept(ideal)

"Happy Piano" is an educational product dedicated to helping children between the ages of 4 and 6 to read music note and play music. This product is designed to solve children's music theory learning difficulties and boring practicing experience. This educational product is mainly composed of smart piano body, smart music score and holographic projection with electronic pet. The main function of the "Happy Piano" is to help children learn common music knowledge in the process of practicing music. Children can know notes, rest, beat, rhythm from music score when they are practicing. In addition, the electronic pet is able to encourage users by doing cute body movements and voice when they complete a song or play a wrong note. At the same time, the electronic pet can be changed according to the user's preferences.

Intended experience(ideal)

The overall expected experience should be joyful and relaxed and convenient. Specifically, in the stage where the user chooses the music score and chooses the characters according to their preferences, the user feels convenient. When the user clicks the note on the music score or other music marks, the user should feel easy. The users should feel happy when practicing and playing the piano.
