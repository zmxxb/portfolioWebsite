Actual Product& Experience

Final product(actual)

Due to the limitations of technical capabilities and resources, the team's product design is still partially flawed, but the basic prototype of the product has been realized. Specifically, the team's current prototype is able to click a note on a sheet of music to know which key it corresponds to. At the same time, when the user presses the corresponding key, the corresponding key in the score also lights up. In addition, when the user starts to play a note, the electronic pet will dance. At the same time, users can switch the type of pet according to their preferences.

Using experience(actual)

The whole process of using the product function is basically smooth. However, due to the limitation of technology and ability, and as the piano is a high-precision instrument. The press feedback of each key of our piano cannot be the same comapared to the real piano. In addition, the projector can not see the object clearly, so the actual visual experience is hard.

Apart from that, it is more smooth and convenient to recognize the corresponding keys by pressing the notes on the music score. Pressing down the corresponding key, the corresponding note on the score will also emit light easily. The use of buttons to switch the electronic pet is easy.
