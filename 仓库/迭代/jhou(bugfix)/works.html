Product Makeup(What I did)

Brief

Our happy piano products are mainly composed of intelligent piano, intelligent music score and electronic pet. Next,I will introduce what I have done.

Smart piano

In order to ensure the appearence of the piano, our smart piano prototype maintains the proportion and size of the normal piano keys, but reduces the number of keys, from 88 to 10, and retains the most important and commonly used 10 keys in the piano, from C4 to E5. By pressing down the piano key, the leverage behind the piano key is pressed down, so as to achieve the real press feedback. The appearence of the pianos was designed by referencing the real piano. However, because the acrylic board cannot be bended, the back of piano is linear, somewhat similar to keyboard. The whole piano use laser cutting technology, to ensure that the piano looks more elegant.

Inside the smart piano, making a huge space is for the installation of the light strip, and electronic components.

Smart Music Score

At the top of the piano there is a stand space, mainly used for smart music sheet and electronic components. The piano's score is customised, each music score has its own music song. The music score is frosted to make it more aesthetically pleasing. Some holes we made in body are for lines of sensors.

Electronic pet

The electronic pet is mainly composed of laptop screen and holographic pyramid. The holographic projection device is equipped with a huge black box for better display effect.
